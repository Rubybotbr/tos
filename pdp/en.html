Privacy Policy - Kash Bot

By using Kash Bot, you agree to all the Terms of Use.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User Data

User Data includes, but is not limited to, User IDs, User IPs, user emails, profile pictures, usernames, and user tags (“discriminator”). The Kash Team uses User Data as a means to personalize your experience and assist you in any way possible. This usage may include, but is not limited to, debugging information for commands like "suggest" or support information to identify a user, use in message embeds, and logging regarding user and command usage.

1.2 Guild Data

Guild Data includes, but is not limited to, Guild IDs, Guild icons, Guild names, member count, roles, channels, and roles held by a member. Guild Data is used as a further means to personalize your experience and assist you in ways that User Data may not be helpful. This usage may include, but is not limited to, debugging information for commands like "suggest" or support information to identify a guild, use in message embeds, and logging regarding command usage. Additionally, developers may use Guild Data in any way needed, as long as it is not used for malicious purposes. Developers reserve the right to generate an invite to a guild to provide support or determine if the guild is abusing our bots.

1.3 Use and Storage of User Data and Guild Data

The Kash Team reserves the right to use and store User Data and Guild Data in any way necessary for the functionality of our bots. The Kash Team will not use User Data or Guild Data for malicious purposes, only when required for specific commands or features of our bots. User and Guild Data will only be stored in specific situations where it is necessary for the persistence of a feature. This includes, but is not limited to, user currency, event logs, blacklists, and access to specific benefits.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information to provide the Kash Bot service to you, personalizing and improving our services. We may also contact you if necessary. The Kash Team reserves the right to remove and permanently delete your bot content or data, with or without notice, for any reason or no reason.
